About World

Temple of Shadows>- The Temple of shadows is the most sacred place for a Vampillon to visit. Every Saturday, Vampillon from all over gather to offer prayers and gain blessings from loved ones of the past.

Catacombs of Ash- The Catacombs are where the loves ones of the past reside. Urns of all shapes and sizes all have their on spot in each covens section of the catacombs, Vampillon's often visit deceased loved ones and pay their respects.

The Blackened Rose- The Blackened Rose is a tavern that every adult Vampillon has heard of or been to at least one. It is a pleasant social gathering place for Vampillon from any part of the realm.

The Fountain of Offerings- The Fountain of Offerings is the place where Vampillon spend the blessings that they have earned. They offer a blessing and the fouuntain provides them with items in which they dont know they need yet, but will.
